SECTION 1: ABOUT GIGINSURANCE
----------------------------------------------------------------
GigInsurance provides parametric income protection for delivery
partners working on Zepto and Blinkit platforms in India.

Unlike traditional insurance, GigInsurance uses real-time
environmental and government data to automatically approve or
deny claims — no paperwork, no waiting, no human adjudication.

When a qualifying event is detected, payouts are processed
within 24 hours directly to your registered UPI account.

SECTION 2: WHO IS ELIGIBLE
----------------------------------------------------------------
You are eligible for GigInsurance if you:
- Are an active delivery partner on Zepto or Blinkit
- Have a valid Partner ID (ZPT### for Zepto, BLK### for Blinkit)
- Have completed GigInsurance onboarding via WhatsApp
- Have an active weekly policy (renews every 7 days)
- Were on an active shift at the time of the disruption

Waiting Period: Coverage begins 48 hours after policy activation.
New workers must wait 48 hours before filing any claim.

SECTION 4: COVERAGE — QUALIFYING DISRUPTION EVENTS
----------------------------------------------------------------
GigInsurance covers the following six disruption types:

4.1 HEAVY RAINFALL
    Trigger:  Rainfall ≥ 50mm in any 6-hour window
    Data:     Open-Meteo Weather API (real-time)
    Payout:   ₹250 for partial disruption (3–6 hrs)
              ₹500 for full-day disruption (6+ hrs)
    Note:     Light drizzle or rainfall below 50mm does NOT qualify.
              Rainfall between 25–50mm may qualify for ₹150 partial
              payout if shift was active for less than 2 hours.

4.2 FLOODING / WATERLOGGING
    Trigger:  Rainfall ≥ 30mm in any 3-hour window
    Data:     Open-Meteo Weather API (real-time)
    Payout:   ₹300 for partial disruption
              ₹500 for full-day disruption
    Note:     Flood claims require rainfall confirmation. Self-reported
              flooding without weather data confirmation will be denied.

4.3 EXTREME HEAT
    Trigger:  Temperature ≥ 45°C during active shift hours (10am–4pm)
    Data:     Open-Meteo Weather API (real-time)
    Payout:   ₹200 per qualifying day
    Note:     Heat claims are only valid between 10am and 4pm.
              Evening heat does not qualify. Temperature must be
              recorded at your delivery zone, not nearest city.

4.4 SEVERE AIR QUALITY (AQI)
    Trigger:  European AQI ≥ 300 (Hazardous) during shift hours
    Data:     Open-Meteo Air Quality API (real-time)
    Payout:   ₹250 per qualifying day
    Note:     AQI between 200–299 (Very Poor) does NOT qualify.
              Only Hazardous (300+) AQI levels trigger a payout.
              Common in Delhi NCR during winter months (Oct–Feb).

4.5 CYCLONE / SEVERE STORM
    Trigger:  IMD-issued cyclone warning OR wind speed ≥ 60 km/h
    Data:     Government cyclone advisory / IMD alerts
    Payout:   ₹500 automatic payout (no weather check required)
    Note:     Cyclone warnings are auto-approved. No manual review.
              Payout is issued immediately upon claim submission
              during an active cyclone warning period.

4.6 CURFEW / SECTION 144
    Trigger:  Government-issued curfew or Section 144 in your zone
    Data:     Government news feeds and official announcements
    Payout:   ₹400 per qualifying day
    Note:     Curfew must be officially declared. Bandh calls by
              political parties without government order do NOT qualify.
              Night curfews (10pm–5am) only pay ₹150.

SECTION 5: DAILY MAXIMUM PAYOUT
----------------------------------------------------------------
Maximum payout per day: ₹500

If multiple disruption events occur on the same day (e.g., heavy

SECTION 6: HOW TO FILE A CLAIM
----------------------------------------------------------------
Filing a claim is simple — just message GigInsurance on WhatsApp:

Step 1: Send a message describing what disrupted your work
        Examples:
        "It was raining heavily and I couldn't deliver"
        "Cyclone warning in my area today"
        "AQI was terrible, very bad smog"
        "Curfew was declared in my zone"

Step 2: GigInsurance automatically:
        - Verifies your active shift status
        - Checks real-time weather/environment data
        - Applies your policy rules
        - Makes a decision in under 30 seconds

Step 3: You receive a decision message with:
        - Claim ID (e.g., CLM-2026-00142)
        - Decision: Approved or Denied
        - Payout amount (if approved)
        - Reason for decision

Step 4: If approved, payout credited to your UPI within 24 hours

SECTION 7: CLAIM DENIAL REASONS
----------------------------------------------------------------
Common reasons claims are denied:

1. INSUFFICIENT WEATHER DATA
   The weather API showed conditions below the trigger threshold.
   Example: You reported heavy rain but API showed only 12mm rainfall.
   Solution: File again during or immediately after the event.

2. SHIFT NOT ACTIVE
   Our records show you were not on an active delivery shift.
   Solution: Ensure your shift status is updated in your platform app.

3. WAITING PERIOD
   Your policy was activated less than 48 hours ago.
   Solution: Wait 48 hours after activation before filing claims.

4. DUPLICATE CLAIM
   A claim was already filed for the same date and event.
   Solution: Only one claim per disruption event per day.

5. POLICY EXPIRED
   Your weekly policy has expired and was not renewed.
   Solution: Premium payment failed — check your UPI and retry.

6. EVENT NOT COVERED
   The disruption type is not covered under your policy.
   Example: Vehicle breakdown, personal illness, traffic jams.
   Solution: These events are outside GigInsurance coverage scope.

SECTION 8: WHAT IS NOT COVERED (EXCLUSIONS)
----------------------------------------------------------------
GigInsurance does NOT cover:
- Vehicle breakdown or punctures
- Personal illness or injury
- Traffic jams or road congestion (without government restriction)
- Platform app downtime or technical issues
- Low order availability
- Personal disputes with customers
- Voluntary non-delivery
- Events occurring outside active shift hours
- Events in zones outside your registered delivery zone

SECTION 9: PAYOUT PROCESS
----------------------------------------------------------------
Approved payouts are processed as follows:
- Approval notification: Immediate (within 30 seconds of claim)
- UPI credit: Within 24 hours of approval
- Bank transfer (if UPI fails): 3–5 business days

UPI ID is collected during onboarding. To update your UPI,
contact support at support@giginsurance.in

SECTION 10: POLICY RENEWAL
----------------------------------------------------------------
Your policy automatically renews every 7 days (Monday to Sunday).
Premium is auto-debited every Monday at 9:00 AM.

If premium payment fails:
- Day 1: Payment retry + WhatsApp notification
- Day 2: Second retry
- Day 3: Policy suspended (claims not accepted)
- Day 7: Policy cancelled

To reactivate a suspended policy, pay the pending premium via
the payment link sent to your WhatsApp.

SECTION 11: FRAUD PREVENTION
----------------------------------------------------------------
GigInsurance uses automated fraud detection:
- Multiple claims in short succession are flagged
- Claims filed outside shift hours are denied
- Weather data is cross-verified with multiple sources
- Accounts with >3 fraudulent claim attempts are suspended

Filing a false claim is a violation of these terms and may
result in permanent account suspension.

SECTION 13: FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
----------------------------------------------------------------
Q: What is parametric insurance?
A: Parametric insurance pays automatically based on data triggers,
   not on actual loss assessment. If rainfall exceeds 50mm, you get
   paid — no proof of lost income needed.

Q: How quickly do I get paid after approval?
A: Payout is credited to your UPI within 24 hours of approval.

Q: Can I file a claim for yesterday's rain?
A: Yes, but only within 24 hours of the event. Claims filed more
   than 24 hours after the event cannot be verified with real-time
   data and will be denied.

Q: What if my area had rain but the threshold wasn't met?
A: Unfortunately, parametric insurance pays only when data thresholds
   are met. 40mm of rainfall does not qualify for the 50mm threshold.
   This is clearly stated in your policy.

Q: Can I change my coverage amount?
A: Coverage amounts are standardized and cannot be customized in the
   current version. Premium tiers with higher coverage (₹750/day,
   ₹1000/day) are coming in Q3 2026.

Q: What if I deliver in multiple zones?
A: Claims are verified against your registered primary zone.
   Contact support to update your zone.

Q: Is GigInsurance IRDAI regulated?
A: GigInsurance operates as a parametric protection product under
   the microinsurance framework. Full IRDAI licensing is in process.

Q: My OTP expired, how do I get a new one?
A: Type "restart" in WhatsApp to begin onboarding again.

Q: How do I check my current policy status?
A: Type "policy" or "my policy" in WhatsApp to see your coverage
   details, validity, and premium amount.

Q: Can I pause my policy during vacation?
A: Policy pausing is not available currently. Contact support for
   manual suspension to avoid premium charges.
